Data Journalism: Visualizing Multi-Axis Data Using D3

Data Field Breakout

Y-Axis

  • Cannot afford Healthcare: % of population which cannot afford healthcare.
  • Unemployment: % of population older than 16 and unemployed.
  • Depression: % of population with known depression.

X-Axis

  • Educaton < Highschool: % of population with less than a high school education.
  • Education > Bachelor: % of population with less than a Bachelors Degree.
  • No Health Coverage: % of population with no healthcare coverage.
